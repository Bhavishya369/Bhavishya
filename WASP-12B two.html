Features of WASP-12b

  • Hot Jupiter

    WASP-12b is a gas giant with extremely high temperatures due to its proximity to its star.

  • Short Orbital Period

    It completes one orbit around its star in just 1.1 days, one of the shortest known orbital periods.

  • Extreme Tidal Forces

    The planet is being distorted by tidal forces as it orbits so close to its host star.

  • Atmospheric Research

    Scientists study WASP-12b's atmosphere for signs of chemical elements and compounds.
