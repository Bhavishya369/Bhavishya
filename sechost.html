Exoplanets with Diverse Host Stars

Proxima b

Proxima b orbits the red dwarf star Proxima Centauri, the closest star to our solar system.

TRAPPIST-1e

TRAPPIST-1e orbits an ultracool dwarf star and is one of seven Earth-sized planets in its system.

Kepler-452b

Kepler-452b orbits a G-type star, often called Earth's cousin due to its similar conditions.

Orbits a G-type star!

HD 209458b

HD 209458b orbits a G-type star and was the first exoplanet discovered to transit its star.

First exoplanet discovered in transit!

WASP-12b is a gas giant exoplanet known for its scorching temperatures, orbiting a G-type star.
