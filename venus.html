Features:

  • Second planet from the Sun, often referred to as Earth's "sister planet."
  • Known for its scorching hot surface with temperatures high enough to melt lead.
  • Thick atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, creating a strong greenhouse effect.
  • Surface features include volcanoes, vast plains, and highland regions.
  • Named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.
