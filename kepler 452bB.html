Features of Kepler-452b

  • Habitable Zone

    Located in the habitable zone of its star, Kepler-452, allowing potential for liquid water.

  • Super-Earth

    Approximately 1.5 times the size of Earth, making it a "super-Earth" exoplanet.

  • Close Proximity

    About 1,400 light-years away from Earth, making it a prime target for exploration.

  • Potential for Life

    Conditions on Kepler-452b may support life, sparking significant scientific interest.
