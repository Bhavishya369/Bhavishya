Explore Gliese 667 Cc

A Potential Earth-Like World!

Features of Gliese 667 Cc:

  • Located in the habitable zone of its star, Gliese 667 C.
  • Approximately 4.5 times the mass of Earth.
  • Potential for liquid water due to its optimal distance from its star.
  • About 23.62 light-years away from Earth.
  • One of the best candidates for habitability among known exoplanets.

Description:

Gliese 667 Cc is a super-Earth exoplanet located in the habitable zone of the red dwarf star Gliese 667 C. Its position allows for the possibility of liquid water, making it a key target in the search for extraterrestrial life.
