Features of K2-18b

  • Habitable Zone

    K2-18b is located in the habitable zone of its star, K2-18, potentially allowing for liquid water.

  • Size and Composition

    Approximately 2.6 times the size of Earth, offering a unique environment for exploration.

  • Water Vapor Presence

    First exoplanet where water vapor was detected in its atmosphere, making it a prime candidate for life.

  • Proximity to Earth

    About 124 light-years away, making it one of the closer exoplanets suitable for detailed study.

  • Potential for Life

    Conditions on K2-18b may support liquid water and, potentially, life forms.
