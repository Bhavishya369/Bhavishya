Features:

  • Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system.
  • It is a gas giant and is often referred to as an "ice giant" due to its icy and gaseous composition.
  • Neptune has a dynamic atmosphere with high-speed winds and a distinctive dark storm known as the Great Dark Spot.
  • The planet has a collection of moons, including Triton, which features geysers erupting from its surface.
  • Neptune has been explored by NASA's Voyager 2 spacecraft, providing valuable insights into this distant world.
