Features of KELT-9b

  • Extreme Temperatures

    KELT-9b boasts surface temperatures exceeding 4,300 K (over 4,000 °C), making it one of the hottest exoplanets discovered.

  • Short Orbital Period

    With an orbital period of just 1.5 days, KELT-9b completes a full orbit around its host star rapidly.

  • Close Proximity

    Located approximately 650 light-years away in the constellation Cygnus, KELT-9b is a prime target for observation.

  • Potential for Research

    Study the atmospheric dynamics and extreme conditions of a "hot Jupiter" to gain insights into planetary formation and behavior.

    Experience a fully immersive virtual exploration of KELT-9b’s surface and atmospheric phenomena.
