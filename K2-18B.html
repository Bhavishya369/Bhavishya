Features of K2-18b

  • Habitable Zone

    K2-18b is located in the habitable zone of its star, K2-18, potentially allowing for liquid water.

  • Size and Composition

    Approximately 2.6 times the size of Earth, indicating a substantial atmosphere.

  • Water Vapor Detection

    First exoplanet where water vapor was detected in its atmosphere, a key indicator of habitability.

  • Proximity

    About 124 light-years away, making it one of the closer exoplanets to Earth.

  • Potential for Life

    Conditions on K2-18b may support liquid water, a fundamental requirement for life as we know it.
