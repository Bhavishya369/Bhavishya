Super-Earths

55 Cancri e

A super-Earth with a molten surface, orbiting its star closely, located 40 light-years away.

55 Cancri e is extremely hot!

Gliese 667 Cc

This exoplanet, 23.62 light-years away, is located in the habitable zone of a red dwarf star.

Gliese 667 Cc might support life!

Kepler-22b

Kepler-22b is a super-Earth that orbits within the habitable zone of its star, 600 light-years away.

Kepler-22b is a larger Earth-like planet!

K2-18b

This super-Earth, located 124 light-years away, shows signs of water vapor in its atmosphere.

K2-18b has water vapor!

Ross 128 b

Ross 128 b is located 11 light-years away and orbits a quiet red dwarf star, potentially habitable.

Ross 128 b could support life!
