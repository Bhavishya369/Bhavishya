Features of HD 209458b

  • First Transiting Exoplanet

    HD 209458b is known as "Osiris," the first exoplanet discovered transiting its star.

  • Hot Jupiter

    This gas giant is located approximately 159 light-years away, orbiting its star every 3.5 days.

  • Atmospheric Studies

    Research indicates the presence of sodium and carbon compounds in its atmosphere.

  • Research Opportunities

    Participate in research that sheds light on atmospheric phenomena and exoplanetary conditions.
