Proxima b

Proxima b is located in the habitable zone of the closest star, Proxima Centauri, just 4.2 light-years away.

One of the seven Earth-like planets orbiting TRAPPIST-1, it lies in the star's habitable zone.

Kepler-452b may be Earth-like!
